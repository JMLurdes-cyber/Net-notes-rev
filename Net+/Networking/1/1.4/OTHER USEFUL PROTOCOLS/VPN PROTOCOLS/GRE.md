- GRE protocol, a.k.a. **Generic Routing Encapsulation** is a protocol that creates a link between two networks. That link allows for packets to be directly transmitted between them, ignoring packet restrictions or distance.
- Every packet being sent through it is **plaintext**.
- It is a foundational protocol in [[VPN|VPNs]]
