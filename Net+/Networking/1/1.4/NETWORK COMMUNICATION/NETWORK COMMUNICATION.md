There are many kinds of communications used depending on the purpose that the admin wants to use the network for.
- [[UNICAST|Unicast]] is chosen for *reliability and possibility of personalization*.
- [[MULTICAST|Multicast]] is chosen for *bandwith efficiency in one-to-many scenarios*.
- [[Anycast]] is chosen for *low latetncy and high availabilty*.
- [[BROADCAST|Broadcast]] (mostly deprecated) used to *send every connected user information at once*.
