- SIP a.k.a. **Session Initiation Protocol** is used to manage Voice over IP functions.
- Useful for video conferences and messages.
- Usually uses two ports:
	- One port takes care of the **signaling**, or in other words, setup, management and tear-down. *Plaintext*
	- Another port takes care of the **media**, in other words, the voice/vide data itself. *Encrypted*. 
### Ports used
- It uses the port [[TCP]] 5060 for **signaling**.
- It uses the port [[TCP]] 5061 for **media**.
