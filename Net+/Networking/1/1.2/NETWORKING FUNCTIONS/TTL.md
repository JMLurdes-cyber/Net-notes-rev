
- **TIME TO LIVE** or TTL is a countdown which specifies the moment that a packet should be dropped off the network.
-  It is found on the [[BASICS OF ENCAPSULATION|IP header]] of the packet.
- The countdown is counted on either router hops (decreasing each time it passes through a router).
