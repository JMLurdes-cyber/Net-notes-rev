
- **VIRTUAL PRIVATE NETWORKS** or VPNs create secure, encrypted connections over the internet, allowing secure remote access and making devices appear inside another network.
- Through the use of their tunneling protocols they can allow a client to connect remotely with the server.
- They also hide the client's original IP.

### Use with VPCs
- Clients can use them to remotely connect to the [[VIRTUAL PRIVATE CLOUDS|VPC]]
- Also useful on hybrid set-ups
