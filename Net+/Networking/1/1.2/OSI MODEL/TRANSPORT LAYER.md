- [[UNDERSTANDING THE OSI MODEL|OSI Model]] Layer 4.
- Manages end to end transfer of data.
- Can be reliable through the use of [[TCP AND UDP|TCP]]
- Can be fast, though unreliable through the use of [[TCP AND UDP|UDP]]
- Can use error control.

### [[BASICS OF ENCAPSULATION|Encapsulation]]
- Adds a[[BASICS OF ENCAPSULATION|TCP header]] for reliable transfers or an [[BASICS OF ENCAPSULATION|UDP header]] for fast transfers
- Provides [[INTRODUCTION TO PORTS|ports]] and reliability information
