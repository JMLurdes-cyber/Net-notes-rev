- [[UNDERSTANDING THE OSI MODEL|OSI Model]] Layer 2.
- Handles communications within the LAN through the use of [[MAC addressing]].
- Uses MAC Addressing and [[BASICS OF ENCAPSULATION|frames]] to deliver data to the correct device.

### [[BASICS OF ENCAPSULATION|Encapsulation]]
- Adds an **Ethernet header** and an **Ethernet trailer**.
- The header takes care of MAC addressing.
- The trailer takes care of error correction.
