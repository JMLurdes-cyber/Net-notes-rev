- Separated on [[DATA LINK LAYER|2nd layer]] and [[NETWORK LAYER|3rd layer]] switches.
- Both forward [[frames]] within a LAN by learning and using the [[MAC Addresses]] of the connected devices and save them into a [[MAC Addressing Table]]
- Only 3rd layer switches can also route using [[IP adresses]], or between [[VLANs]]
