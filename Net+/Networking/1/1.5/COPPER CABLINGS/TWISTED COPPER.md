- 8 cables twisted into 4 pairs.
- Each pair is color-matched:
	- Orange - White
	- Green    -  White
	- Blue       - White
	- Brown   - White
- Each pair sends equal and opposite signals.
- By twisting, both cables are exposed to the same interference, which the receiving computer can subtract from the signal leaving only the data.
- The twist is differential to make each pair not interfere with each other.
- Separated into **categories**, to showcase the minimum category needed to work properly following the [[IEEE| IEEE standard.]]
